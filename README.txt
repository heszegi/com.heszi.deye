A simple App to monitor your Deye Solar System using the DeyeCloud API.

Start and stop devices based on solar power production, battery usage, or other conditions you define in Homey Flows. Take action when your solar system starts producing energy, or receive a notification if the battery becomes depleted.

Homey Energy Integration

App add your solar panels and battery to Homey Energy tab through the inverter device. Monitor real-time solar production, energy charts, battery charging/discharging power, and battery temperature directly in the Energy tab.
